WeFood Free Food For Everyone

A project my best friend and I embarked on was "WeFood," a web design initiative aimed at promoting healthy living. This project stemmed from his college assignment, and knowing my expertise in web development, he enlisted my help. The concept was simple yet impactful: users would engage in various healthy activities suggested by the website, such as walking a certain number of steps or trying new healthy recipes, to earn points redeemable for free food. While he focused on designing the user interface, I handled the coding and implementation of the website's functionalities. Upon completion, he received an outstanding grade for the project, and I was thrilled to have contributed to such a meaningful endeavor.

Westerchire

I collaborated with the renowned IT company "Postponed" on a public web design project named "Westerchire," focusing on travel and exploration. Recognizing my expertise in web development, Postponed enlisted my help for the coding and integration of the website's features. The project was a resounding success, offering a valuable resource for travel enthusiasts, and I was honored to have contributed to such a meaningful endeavor.
